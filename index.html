Our Mission

Empowering girls through menstrual health education and resources.

About Our CEO & Team

Mr. Jubel Sherif is the visionary founder and CEO of Empower Her Africa. As a passionate advocate for youth and girls' empowerment, he is committed to making a lasting impact in Liberia by addressing menstrual health, education, and social support for vulnerable girls. With experience in leadership and community engagement, he drives the mission of Empower Her Africa with dedication and innovation.

Behind him stands a powerful team of passionate individuals who share the same vision. Our team is made up of skilled professionals, volunteers, and advocates working tirelessly to create opportunities for girls through education, mentorship, and sustainable development projects. Together, we are building a future where every girl has the support and resources she needs to thrive.
